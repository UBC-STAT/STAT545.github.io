Prerequisites

While there are no formal pre-requisites for STAT 545 A, students should be familiar with basic statistical analyses and have taken at least one introductory statistics course.

STAT545 A is a pre-requisite for STAT545 B. No exceptions.

Course Structure

This course will feature short lectures and in-class demonstrations so students get hands-on experience with the help of their instructor and TAs. The class will be partially “flipped”, with students being expected to review a short video prior to the lecture. Dedicated time will be given in lecture for students to work through assigned worksheets.

Please bring a charged laptop to every class. If you do not have a personal laptop, one can be borrowed through the UBC library.
